Q) Which string method is used to removes the leading and
   trailing whitespace on strings, including tabs, 
   newlines, and carriage returns (\t, \n, \r).
A) The .strip method removes the leading and trailing 
    whitespace on strings, including tabs, newlines, and 
    carriage returns (\t, \n, \r).
    Syntax: 
        string.strip
    Ex: 
        "karim".strip
